Our target audience includes parents seeking quality early education, young students building foundations, and staff dedicated to nurturing growth.

Admission

Admission Process

  1. Submit online application form (parent interview for Playgroup; simple assessment for Class 1-8).
  2. Attend observation/interview (no formal exam for younger grades).
  3. Receive acceptance letter.
  4. Complete enrollment with documents.

Fee Structure

  • Playgroup: $300/month
  • Classes 1-5: $400/month
  • Classes 6-8: $500/month
  • Registration: $100 (one-time)
  • Extras: $50/month (activities)
